If you use Midbound Platform, you may need to update your own privacy policy to disclose this to your users. Below is suggested language you can adapt for your privacy policy.

---

## Information Collection Through Website Analytics and Marketing Services

### Third-Party Analytics and Identification Services

We use Midbound Inc.'s person-based marketing platform to help us understand how visitors interact with our website and to identify potential business opportunities. Through this service:

**Information Automatically Collected:**

- When you visit our website, Midbound's technology may identify you or your organization by analyzing your site traffic against their data sources
- This may include collection of your IP address, browser type, device information, and browsing behavior on our site
- Midbound may enrich this information with publicly available business contact information such as name, job title, company, industry, LinkedIn profile, email address, and phone number

**Purpose of Collection:**

- To identify companies and individuals visiting our website
- To understand visitor interests and behavior
- To enable us to provide relevant business communications
- To improve our website and services
- To generate leads and business opportunities

---

## Your Rights Regarding This Information

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding the information collected through these services:

- **Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing (California, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, Virginia):** You have the right to opt-out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information. The identification and enrichment services we use may constitute a "sale" or "sharing" under certain state laws.
- **Right to Know/Access:** You may request to know what personal information we have collected about you through these services.
- **Right to Delete:** You may request deletion of personal information we have collected about you, subject to certain exceptions.
- **Right to Correct:** You may request correction of inaccurate personal information we maintain about you.
- **Right to Data Portability:** Where applicable, you may request to receive your personal information in a portable and readily usable format.
- **Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information:** If we collect sensitive personal information as defined under applicable law, you may have the right to limit its use.

| Category | Examples | Purpose |

## Data Retention

Information collected through website analytics and enrichment services is retained according to our data retention policy, typically for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

## Third-Party Service Provider Disclosure

Midbound Inc. acts as our service provider/processor for website visitor identification and enrichment. They process information on our behalf under contractual agreements that limit their use of your information. For more information about Midbound's own privacy practices, please visit [https://legal.midbound.ai/platform/privacy/](/platform/privacy/).
